Follow these instructions to download and play the "Catch the blue button" game:

Download the game file called "Catch the blue button.sb3" to your computer - right-click here and select "Save link as...".

Open https://scratch.mit.edu/projects/editor/?tutorial=getStarted in a new browser window.

Click "File", then click "Load from your computer", and find the game file you downloaded in the first step.

Clicking the green flag starts the game. Use the arrow keys to move. Catch the blue button and avoid everything else.
